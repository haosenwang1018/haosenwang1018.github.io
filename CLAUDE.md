# Personal Website Rules

This is Sense Wang's personal website. Preserve the current homepage feel: single-screen, centered, minimal, serif, black-and-white, and non-scrollable unless the user explicitly asks to redesign it.

Writing pages may scroll, but should stay visually quiet and essay-like. When creating or editing technical blog posts, use the global `technical-blog-craft` skill: prefer one concrete mechanism or bottleneck, primary sources, compact technical depth, and a voice that shows sincere progress rather than broad hype summaries.

The writing section should support high-cadence compounding, not one fixed house style. Posts may be short notes, long essays, diagrams, paper-detail cuts, implementation notes, or Twitter/X-style observations. Keep the stable bar: sincere learning, technical grounding, useful mental models, and selective references to strong writers such as Paul Graham, Sam Altman, Andrej Karpathy, Su Jianlin, Nathan Chen, Tri Dao, Simon Willison, Chip Huyen, Eugene Yan, and Anthropic/Distill.
